TEDxJU 2025 was an independently organized TED event hosted at the University of Jordan. As the lead graphic designer, I developed a complete visual identity system for the event — from the core logo and brand guidelines to all print and digital materials.

## The Challenge

<!-- TODO: Layan to fill in challenge description -->

The University of Jordan TEDx chapter needed a fresh, bold visual identity that honoured the global TED brand while expressing the unique energy of the 2025 event theme.

## The Process

<!-- TODO: Layan to fill in process description -->

I began with extensive research into the event's theme, then developed multiple logo concepts before landing on the final direction. From there I built out the full system: color palette, typography, iconography, and layout templates for all collateral.
